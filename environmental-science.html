  ABOUT US

"Soil Book" is an informational and research-based organization (soilbook.org) in Bangladesh that was established in 2020. Mahmudul Hasan Sumon is the founder of the organization. It spread environmental knowledge by writing and sharing conceptual articles related to Soil Science, Environmental Science, Disaster Management and Environmental News as a part of rising Environmental Awareness.
